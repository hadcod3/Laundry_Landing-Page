About Us

Welcome to Had Laundry, your trusted partner for all your laundry needs! We understand that life can get busy, and laundry often falls to the bottom of your to-do list. That’s where we come in. Our mission is to make laundry day effortless, convenient, and stress-free. Whether it’s your everyday clothes, delicate fabrics, or bulky bedding, we handle it all with care, precision, and a commitment to quality. Say goodbye to laundry hassles and hello to fresh, clean clothes delivered right to your doorstep.

At Had Laundry, we take pride in offering more than just laundry services – we deliver an experience. Our team of professionals uses state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly detergents to ensure your clothes are cleaned to perfection, every time. From stain removal to fabric softening, we pay attention to the smallest details so you can enjoy the confidence of wearing spotless, fresh-smelling clothes. With flexible scheduling, affordable pricing, and a customer-first approach, we’re here to make your life easier and your wardrobe brighter.

We believe that clean clothes shouldn’t come at the cost of your time or the environment. That’s why we’ve designed our services to be fast, reliable, and sustainable. Whether you’re a busy professional, a parent juggling multiple responsibilities, or someone who simply values their free time, Had Laundry is here to support you. Let us take care of the laundry while you focus on what truly matters. Experience the difference today – because you and your clothes deserve the best!

Our System

Five Easy Steps to Spotless Clothes.

Administration

Admin

Our admin team ensures everything runs smoothly. They manage orders, coordinate schedules, and handle customer inquiries to provide a seamless experience from start to finish.

Washing Stage

Laundry Attendant

Our skilled laundry attendants carefully sort and wash your clothes using the latest technology and eco-friendly detergents. Every garment is treated with care to ensure a thorough and gentle clean.

Drying Stage

Drying Clerk

After washing, our drying clerks ensure your clothes are dried perfectly. Using advanced drying techniques, they preserve fabric quality while removing every trace of moisture.

Ironing Stage

Ironing Officer

Our ironing officers give your clothes a crisp, polished finish. With precision and attention to detail, they ensure every fold and crease is perfect, leaving your garments looking brand new.

Packaging Stage

Packing Officer

At the final stage, our packing officers carefully fold and package your clothes. They ensure everything is neatly organized and ready for delivery, so your laundry arrives fresh and pristine.
